Indomie

My sweet Nigerian made indomie

I made this delicious indomie for Awwal Saidu, Jahzrock damilola Ojo and Hauwa sule

it's nice. I made it with a touch of pepper soup and some nice shaki on it

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • maggi
  • Onion
  • Pepper
  • Pepper soup
  • Beef
  • Onga

Steps

  1. Boil your water
  2. Put your noodles inside of the boiling water
  3. pour in your ingredients
  4. pour in your pepper soup
  5. pour in your Meat
  6. wait for like 30mins and then you are good
